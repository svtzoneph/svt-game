About the Flip the Card SVT

This fan-made game is made for CARATS🃏💎 Flip cards to match each member and test your memory skills.

Tap any card to flip it, then flip another to try and match the pair. If they match, they stay open. If not, they'll flip back — remember their spots!

⏱️ A timer records how long you take to complete each level — try to finish as fast as you can!

🎯 A move counter tracks how many moves you’ve made — challenge yourself to complete the game with fewer flips!

There’s no time limit or move limit, so you can enjoy the game at your own pace.

💖 This game is completely FREE to play — enjoy and have fun, Carats!

This version is currently in test run, so you're getting an early preview of the gameplay. More updates are coming soon!
